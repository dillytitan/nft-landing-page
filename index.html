NFT DROP COMING SOON!!!

- My Instagram
 

The thought that I could create a whole world with pictures, animations and music all into one piece was so captivating. It really pushes the limits of art and being creative. It’s literally anything you can think of and make happen. Web3 technology has allowed us to take these ideas and make them a reality.

On paper, or skin sometimes can feel limiting, or be void of emotion. But, when you have an interactive piece of art you tend not to forget as fast, like a movie or a ride at an amusement park. I love the feeling you get when you see something and you just think, “ how the hell did they do that?” That feeling strikes emotion, more importantly, it strikes the emotion to create and inspire. That’s all I ever want to come out of my art. The ability to strike emotion and inspire others to create something even better. Whether it's art or graphics, music, or a screenplay. Creating inspiration to keep creating is my true passion.

For the last decade, tattooing has been my medium to showcase this passion. I’m so grateful for it, collaborating with another person every day to create something unique and one of a kind is an absolute blessing. I’m honored to have such a privilege.

With that said, I’ve always wanted more, I always wished tattoos could move or have a life of their own. This project is my opportunity to create highly inspiring, interactive art for the world to enjoy.
