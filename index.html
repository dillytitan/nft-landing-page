NFT Drop Coming Soon!!

>Project: Stephen Sanchez NFT Project

>Drop Date: 2022.04.08

>Bio:

Tattooing has been my medium for the last decade. I’m so grateful for it, collaborating with another person every day to create something unique and one of kind is an absolute blessing. I’m honored to have such a privilege. With that said, I’ve always wanted more, I always pondered “I wish these could move or have a life of their own.” Or “ how can these be more interactive and still strike emotion ?” Well, that’s when I was introduced to NFTS. The thought that I can create a whole world with pictures animations and music all into one piece was so captivating and really pushes the limits of art and being creative. It’s literally anything you can think of and make happen. Technology has allowed us to take these ideas and make them a reality. On paper or skin sometimes can feel limiting or not even strike emotion. But when you have an interactive piece of art you tend not to forget as fast, kinda like a movie or an amusement ride. I love that feeling you get when you see something and you just think, “ how the hell did they do that?” That feeling strikes emotion, more importantly, it strikes the emotion to create and inspire. That’s all I ever want to come out of my art, is to strike emotion to inspire and create something even better. Whether it's more art or graphics, music a screenplay, etc. Creating inspiration to keep creating.
